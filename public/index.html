Publications

Attacks on Elliptic Curve Cryptography Implementations in Sage Math
International Journal of Innovative Science and Research Technology 5 May 2023
Pagilla Manohar Reddy

This paper presents a summary of the various attacks that have been discovered on the implementation of Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC) in SageMath, a widely used open-source mathematics software. ECC is a popular method for providing secure communication and is used in a variety of applications, such as secure key exchange, digital signatures, and more. However, the implementation of ECC in real life has been found to have several vulnerabilities that could potentially be exploited by attackers. This paper will look at the various attacks that have been found and how they affect the safety of ECC-based systems.

Discrete Log Problem Elliptic Curve Cryptography Pohlig Hellman Singular Curve Smart Attack MOV Attack ECDLP Digital Signature
